Introduction

This project was completed during my time at school as part of a capstone project. The goal was to connect multiple Raspberry Pi devices with PCs and mobile devices to implement the transmission and display of sound recognition results. The core of the project was running a sound recognition model on Raspberry Pi or a PC, then storing the recognition results on a PC, and finally displaying them on client devices (such as phones and smartwatches). This system was specifically designed for people with hearing impairments, helping them stay informed about what’s happening in their homes by placing multiple pi-devices throughout their residence. In this project, I independently completed the design and development of the frontend application, as well as the integration of data transmission and storage.
